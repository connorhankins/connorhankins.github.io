About Me


I am a recent graduate of the Indiana University Kelley School of Business with an interest in further developing my database and programming skills through a co-major in technology management. Soon, I will be joining the Microsoft Finance Rotation Program (FRP) where I will develop a variety of finance skills while working at one of the world’s prominent companies.

My previous experience includes working at AbbVie Biopharmaceuticals as a Finance Intern in their Business Technology Solutions division where I received the AbbVie Excellence award for an innovative internship project. Moreover, I interned at Microsoft in their highly regarded FRP internship program where I developed an advanced PowerBI dashboard that was utilized by over 40 colleagues on my team and up to 350 business partners.

Beyond a strong understanding of corporate finance and capital markets, I possess a variety of soft skills such as public speaking, leadership and teamwork that I continually attempt to develop. My public speaking skills were on display during my Microsoft internship as I was selected to discuss my internship experience during Microsoft Intern Day in front of 3,000 of my intern colleagues (Click Here for Video).

Outside of the classroom, I am passionate about further developing my communication skills through my involvement in WIUX college radio, where I have produced both business-talk shows and sports-talk programs. To give back to the Kelley School, I have volunteered to be a peer mentor for first-year Kelley students and have utilized my extensive accounting background to be an accounting peer-coach. Skills that I have excelled in and am continually working to master are:

  • Presentation Delivery
  • Data Analytics
  • Leadership
  • Tableau
  • Excel Data Modeling
  • MS PowerBI
  • SAP
  • VBA
  • HTML/CSS
I possess an enthusiasm to learn new things while having an endless drive to pursue my passions. In my free time, I enjoy listening to new podcasts and playing on an intramural basketball team. Looking to the future, I am excited to begin my career at Microsoft and work towards the company’s mission of empowering every person and every organization on the planet to achieve more.
